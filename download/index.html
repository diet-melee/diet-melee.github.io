Download Guides

You do not need to be a Patron on Patreon to access the download; Patrons just get the newer builds of Diet Melee a bit sooner than everyone else. If you would like to help us, we would very much appreciate your support.


Both sets of instructions will make a new iso, and not replace or overwrite your vanilla iso. Your normal Melee is safe.


How to install on Windows:

1. Download Diet Melee Alpha 3
2. Extract the main folder, DietMeleeAlpha3
3. Open Dolphin, the one you use for rollback/slippi(and make sure its up to date), and right click your vanilla Melee iso, then click “open containing folder”. See where your vanilla ISO is stored in your computer.
4. Once you’ve found your Melee ISO, drag it on to “Drag your NTSC 1.02 ISO here.bat”
5. It will generate DietMelee.iso in the Diet Melee folder if everything went right.
6. Put this iso in the same folder as your vanilla Melee iso, and then refresh dolphin. Diet Melee should show up as a separate ISO.
You should be good to go, and it should be compatible with unranked, direct, and doubles.
If you still have any problems, make sure to first check the FAQ



How to install on Mac:

1. Download MultiPatch
2. Download Diet Melee Alpha 3
3. Extract the main folder, DietMeleeAlpha3
3a (optional; This step is just for finding your vanilla ISO, if you know where it is, skip it.) Open Dolphin, the one you use for rollback/slippi(and make sure its updated), and right click your vanilla Melee iso, then click “open containing folder”. See where your vanilla ISO is stored in your computer.
4. Open MultiPatch
5. For the first field, find “patch.xdelta” in the DietMeleeAlpha3 folder.
6. Second field, put in your vanilla ISO you just found in your Dolphin.
7. Third field, browse to the folder your vanilla ISO is in, then put in DietMeleeAlpha3.iso. Click Save.
8. Patch.
You’re good to go and have Diet Melee installed on Mac!
If you still have any problems, make sure to first check the FAQ
